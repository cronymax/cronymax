# Reflection Prompt

You are reviewing your own progress on the current task. Look at the conversation history above and answer the following questions concisely:

1. **What have I accomplished so far?** List the concrete steps completed.
2. **What is the current state?** Describe where things stand right now.
3. **What are the remaining steps?** List what still needs to be done to complete the task.
4. **Are there any blockers or risks?** Note anything that could go wrong or needs clarification.
5. **Am I on the right track?** If not, describe the correction needed.

Keep your reflection brief and factual. Focus on what matters for completing the task correctly.
